Flash the Printward Scale

This installs the Printward firmware on the load-cell scale (ESP32-S3). It runs entirely in your browser — nothing to download or install.

Plug the scale into this computer with a USB-C cable, then click:

Your browser can't do this — use Chrome or Edge on a computer (not a phone). This page needs to be opened over https.

Steps

  1. Use Chrome or Edge on a computer (phones and Firefox/Safari can't talk to USB devices).
  2. Connect the scale with a USB-C cable.
  3. Click Connect & Install, pick the scale's port from the list, and confirm. Flashing takes about a minute.
  4. When it's done, the scale creates its own Wi-Fi network for first-time setup — connect to it and enter your home Wi-Fi, then calibrate with a known weight. See the scale guide.

No port in the list? Try a different USB-C cable (some are charge-only), or hold the scale's BOOT button while plugging it in.
